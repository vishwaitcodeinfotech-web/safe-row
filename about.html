OUR TRADE TERM

FLEXIBLE PAYMENT & SHIPPING SOLUTIONS

Accepted Incoterms

EXW – Ex-Works

Maximum control for you. We make the goods available at our warehouse; you handle the rest.

FOB - Free On Board

We handle transport and customs up to the Indian port; you manage the ocean freight.

CIF – Cost, Insurance & Freight

A hassle-free option. We cover the costs, Insurance, and freight to your destination port.

FCA – Free Carrier

We deliver the goods, cleared for export, to a carrier nominated by you.

DDP – Delivered Duty Paid

Complete peace of mind. We deliver the goods directly to your doorstep, handling all duties.

Flexible Payment Options

Speed & Simplicity (100% Advance)

Samples or small air-freight orders. Ensures the fastest dispatch.

Secure Trade (30% Adv. & 70% Irrevocable L/C)

Large bulk orders. Secure for both parties using a Letter of Credit.

Balanced Terms (50% Adv. & 50% Against Scanned B/L)

Established partners. You pay the balance after seeing proof of shipment (Bill of Lading).

Document Payment (40% Adv. & 60% DP)

Sea shipments where documents are routed through the bank.

Note: All payment terms are negotiable based on order volume, destination, and long-term client relationship.
